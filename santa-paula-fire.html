Santa Paula Fire: Urgent Evacuations in Ventura County Amid Red Flag Warning

June 2024 · 7 minute read

Santa Paula Fire: A brush fire has erupted on South Mountain Road in Ventura County, triggering evacuations and causing widespread concern. The fire broke out during a Red Flag warning, adding to the urgency of the situation. As Ventura County Fire and other units battle the blaze, residents in the affected areas have been ordered to evacuate for their safety. With strong winds exacerbating the fire, air tankers and helicopters have been deployed to combat the rapidly spreading flames. The ongoing Santa Ana Wind Event has prompted the National Weather Service to issue a Red Flag Warning for Ventura County. Southern California Edison is also monitoring electrical circuits for potential power shutoffs. Stay safe and follow the instructions of local authorities.
