Chad Mark Reed AM (born 15 March 1982 in Kurri Kurri, Australia) is an Australian motocross and supercross rider who competes in the World Supercross Championship. He has won many championships in both supercross and motocross. He was raised with the expectation that he would be able to compete at the highest level in his sport from an early age. Over the decade 2000-2009, he has proved to be the best consistent supercross and motocross rider on the planet. Since then, he has progressed up the international levels of the sport, becoming Australia’s most successful motocross racer to date.
