Imaan Is A Supermodel; Know Her Net Worth

Imaan Hammam, who is widely recognized as a cover girl in the British Vogue alongside Taylor Hills and Anna Ewers is a supermodel. She was fourteen when she got scouted by an agent from CODE Management in 2010.

After a couple of years, she made her debut in Givenchy show in 2012 and later got featured in eight other issues of Vogue. She also signed with DNA Model Management in 2012.

In 2016, Imaan won the Couturesque Magazine's Model of the Year competition beating a fellow supermodel, Bella Hadid. Similarly, she worked with Coach, TopShop, H&M, and Celine.

Imaan, who appeared in international magazines like American Vogue, Teen Vogue, and Vogue Netherlands, has walked the runway for the big-profiled designers like Marc Jacobs, Alexander McQueen, and Burberry. Currently, she is signed with Viva Model Management, Why Not Model Management, Code Model Management, and DNA Management.

She was born in Amsterdam, in Netherland in 1996 and she celebrates her birthday on 5 October. Along with her modeling career, she has starred in movies like Nude Area (2014) and Secret Life of Flowers (2017).
