On This Day, December 27, Is Costco Open?

Families may wish to visit Costco after the Christmas Day celebrations to acquire any extra products in bulk. But on December 26, is Costco open?

Opening hours have been constrained as a result of the holiday closure of many businesses.

So here is everything you need to know about Costco on December 26 in case you need to make a grocery run.

Is Costco Open on December 27?

Yes, Costco will be open on December 27 as usual.

Despite being closed on Christmas Day, Costco is open on December 26 during regular business hours from Monday through Friday.

The store will be open from 10:00 am to 8:30 pm, giving customers a whole day to fill up any necessities.

You can use the Costco store locator to confirm what time your neighborhood Costco will be open today.

Is Costco Open on New Year’s Day?

The US warehouses will be closed on New Year’s Day, including Costco.

The retail business observes a number of holidays throughout the year, including Thanksgiving Day and the Fourth of July.

However, Costco is open for business as usual for the rest of the year.

Costco December Deals

On top of being open on December 26, Costco also has ongoing promotions.

Appliance holiday savings are currently available at Costco, and their Christmas Day specials deal is still active. Visit their website to make sure you don’t miss any promotions.

You can join Costco as a member to save extra money there. The $60 yearly Gold Star membership is a great value. Or, for $120 a year, you can choose the Executive membership, which offers a 2% annual incentive.
