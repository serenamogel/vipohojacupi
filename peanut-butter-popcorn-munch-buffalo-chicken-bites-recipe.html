    Peanut Butter Popcorn Munch

    Peanut Butter Popcorn Munch is an incredibly delicious way to get your snack on whether it’s for a game day celebration or family movie night.

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword peanut butter popcorn munch Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 1 minute minute Cool 10 minutes minutes Total Time 16 minutes minutes Servings 10 Calories 306kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 3 oz microwave butter popcorn 1 bag
    • 1 cup tiny pretzel twists
    • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 3/4 - 1 cup powdered sugar
    • 1 cup peanut butter M&M's

    Instructions

    • Prepare popcorn according to package directions, removing all unpopped kernels.

    • Combine popped corn and pretzels in large bowl.

    • Microwave peanut butter and butter in small microwave-safe bowl on HIGH 1 to 1-1/2 minutes or until melted; stir to blend.

    • Drizzle mixture evenly over popcorn mixture; toss to coat.

    • Sprinkle confectioners' sugar over popcorn mixture; toss to coat.

    • Cool 10 minutes before serving. Store leftovers in tightly sealed container.
