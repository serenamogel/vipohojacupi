Full-time Twitch streamer and video content creator who has garnered a following of 260,000 subscribers on her self-titled YouTube channel. She has been known to share gameplay content from installments in The Last of Us series, Resident Evil, Elden Ring, Animal Crossing, Bloodborne, Sekiro, Dark Souls 3, and more during her multi-day streams on her aileensenpai Twitch account, which boasts 30,000 followers.

Before Fame

She became a Twitch Affiliate on July 13, 2020. On March 8, 2022, she was named a Twitch Partner. On June 14, 2021, she posted her first YouTube video, which featured her reaction to the song "Pisces" by the Ukranian metalcore band Jinjer, fronted by lead vocalist Tatiana Shmayluk.

Trivia

She is a fervent enthusiast of heavy metal and death core music, with bands like Lorna Shore, Whitechapel, Linkin Park, and Paramore having been listed amongst her favorites. Her YouTube channel has showcased countless videos featuring her thoughts and reactions to various songs in the genre, and she even has series dedicated to songs from bands like Tool, Rammstein, Electric Callboy, and Slipknot.  

Family Life

She shared a photo of herself alongside her mother, Iliana Velazquez, on her aileensenpai Instagram account on June 6, 2017. The following year, on June 17, she posted a photo with her father. Her pet husky Arya has appeared in many of her Twitch streams.

Associated With

She has posted videos on her YouTube channel praising the vocal talents of singers including Cakra Khan from Goo Goo Dolls, pop singer Putri Ariani, and Falling in Reverse front-man Ronnie Radke.
