Shaymin (Team Up 10)

April 2024 · 1 minute read

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Shaymin (Japanese: シェイミ♢ Shaymin♢) is a Grass-type Basic Pokémon card. It was first released as part of the Team Up expansion.

Card text

Pokédex data

Shaymin - Gratitude Pokémon
Pokédex entry
The blooming of Gracidea flowers confers the power of flight upon it. Feelings of gratitude are the message it delivers.
グラシデアの花が 咲く 季節 感謝の 心を 届けるために 飛び立つと 言われている。

Release information

This card was included in the Team Up expansion, first released in the Japanese GX Ultra Shiny subset. It was reprinted in the Japanese Tag All Stars subset.

Trivia

Origin

This card features Shaymin in its Sky Forme. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Y.
