Who is Siddhanth Kapoor?

Siddhanth Kapoor is a Bollywood actor and assistant director from India. He began his career as an assistant director in several films, including the 2006 comedy murder mystery Bhagam Bhag, the comedy drama Chup Chup Ke, and the 2007 comedy Dhol.

He then appeared in Sanjay Gupta’s crime film Shootout at Wadala and Anurag Kashyap’s psychological thriller film Ugly.

Kapoor attended the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute, where he studied filmmaking and acting. He began his career as a disc jockey before becoming an assistant director for director Priyadarshan in several films, including Bhool Bhulaiya, Bhagam Bhag, and Chup Chup Ke.
