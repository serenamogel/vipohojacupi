Who are the Members of Greta Van Fleet?

February 2024 · 4 minute read

The members of Greta Van Fleet band consists of four musicians whose names are Josh Kiszka, Jake Kiszka, Sma Kiszka, and Daniel Kiszka.

For some background information, Greta Van Fleet is an American rock and roll band based in Frankenmuth Michigan, United States. It was formed in 2021 after the band members were invited to perform at a hometown festival. Keep scrolling for more interesting details about the Greta Van Fleet members.

Meet the Four Greta Van Fleet Members

Josh Kiszka 

  • Date of birth: April 23, 1996
  • Age: 27 years old
  • Role: lead vocalist, Frontman

Josh Kiszka has been the lead vocalist and frontman of the Greta Van Fleet from 2021 to date. He was born in Michigan, United States, on 23th April 1996, making him Age: 27 years old.

While growing up, Josh was a big fan of Classic Rock and Roll music, just like his fellow band members. He stands out from other band members because of his high-pitched sonorous voice coupled with his high-spirited pattern of movements while performing on stage. He stands at 5 feet 5 inches, making him the shortest man in the band.

Moreover, his voice has been likened to that of Led Zeppelin lead singer, Robert Plant. The similarity in their voices is so much that many people upon hearing their debut single, “Highway Tune,” mistook it for an unearthed Zeppelin track. In addition to being the lead vocalist of Greta Van Fleet, Josh also plays the tambourine.

Jake Kiszka

  • Date of birth: April 23, 1996.
  • Age: 27 years old
  • Role: Guitarist, backup vocalist

Jake Kiszka is the lead guitarist and founding member of the band. He is also the identical twin brother of Josh Kiszka, as they share the same birth date and were born five minutes apart. Regardless that they are twins, one can easily distinguish him from his twin brother because of his long locks. Jake stands at a height of 5 feet 6 inches, slightly taller than his brother by 1 inch.

Jake started playing the guitar at the age of 3. H also developed a passion for classical Rock at a very young age. That passion snowballed into the desire to be a part of a Rock band. This birthed the entire idea for Greta Van Fleet.

The groundwork for the band was laid while Jake was still in high school where he met Kyle Hauck, who was a former band member. Soon after graduating from Frankenmuth High School, he attended Delta College, and that was when the band started gaining fame.

Sam Kiszka

  • Date of birth: April 3, 1999
  • Age: 24 years old
  • Role: Bassist, Keyboardist, backup vocalist

Sam Kiszka is the younger brother of Jake and Josh Kiszka. He is the bassist and backup vocalist for Greta Van Fleet. Sam was born on April 3, 1999, making him 24 years old. Despite being one of the youngest members of the band, he co-founded the group alongside his brothers. As the younger brother of twins Josh and Jake, he was nudged to play the bass guitar until he honed the skill well enough to play for the group.

Aside from being a talented bassist. Sam had a separate talent away from music, as he is a talented golfer. He started playing while in high school and even made it to state finals. However, he had to quit the game in the state finals to attend a music tour with the band in Sweden. Sam stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches which makes him taller than his elder brothers.

Danny Wegner

  • Date of birth: December 29, 1999.
  • Age: 23 years old
  • Role: Drummer, backup vocalist

Danny Wegner is the drummer of the band. He joined the band in 2013 as the last addition and replacement for Kayle Hauck. He is 23 years old and was born on December 29, 1999. Moreover, he is the tallest man in the band, as he stands at a height of 5 feet 10 inches.

Danny has been a close friend of the Kiszka brothers even before he became an official member of the band. Like the Kiszka brothers, his hometown is Frankenmuth Michigan, United States

Additionally, the drummer comes from a musical family. Besides being a talented drummer, he also plays the guitar. He first started learning the musical instruments with a guitar, which his mother mentored him in. He also has a sister named Rosie. Danny Wegner is also a talented Golfer aside from his musical talents. He played golf alongside his friend, Sam Kiszka, while in high school.
