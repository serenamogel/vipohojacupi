Lunathi Mampofu Biography, Age, Career &amp; Net Worth

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Lunathi Mampofu (born 7 March 1987) is a South African actress, musical artist, and couturière known for her role as Zikhona in the South African drama series, Ingozi.

Lunathi Mampofu Age

She was born on the 7th of March 1987 in Cape Town, South Africa. As of 2022, she is 35 years old and celebrates her birthday on March 7th every year.

Family

Her parents are known as Nosabata and Patrick Mampofu. She has two brothers who are twins and are named Oros Mampofu and Mkhokheli Prince Mampofu.

Education

She attended primary and high school in Cape Town, South Africa. She moved on to the South African Academy of Motion Picture Medium and Live Performance (AFDA) after finishing high school and graduated in 2010. She went on to the New York Film Academy, where she graduated in 2013.

Career

Almost Maine, Remember to Remember the Memorial, and Look Before You Leap are just a few of Lunathi’s theater credits. She’s also been in a number of student and technical films, including AFDA’s Rising Light, Umthandazo, and Little Blue Line, as well as the short film Shack Attack and feature films The Good Man and Mercy.

In 2016, she made her television acting debut in an episode of the Mzansi Magic drama series Isikizi (Season 1, Episode 9), in which she played a spiritualist.

She’s also been in advertisements for Discovery Insure and Glen Tea, among others.

Lunathi has worked as a casting director at Gersh Studios in Los Angeles and later at Scout’s Honour in Seapoint, Cape Town, in addition to acting.

Net Worth

She has an estimated net worth of $300,000 which she has earned as an actress.
