Slow Cooker Gingerbread Mocha

Course Beverage Cuisine American Keyword mocha, slow cooker gingerbread mocha Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 1 hour hour Total Time 1 hour hour 5 minutes minutes Servings 4 servings Calories 227kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chocolate milk
  • 2 cups freshly brewed coffee
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 cup International Delight Gingerbread Latte creamer

Instructions

  • Combine all ingredients into slow cooker and set on low. Stir occasionally to make sure the molasses and ginger dissolve.

  • Low is the perfect setting for me to get a hot but not too hot mug of this mocha. Use the setting that works for you.

  • Top with whipped cream and a dusting of ginger and cinnamon if desired.
