Angelica Jones is a singer actress and politician in the Philippines.

Personal life

Born Angelica B. Alarva on January 20, 1983 in Brgy Santisimo Rosario, San Pablo, Laguna. She is currently a Board Member representing the 3rd district of Laguna.
